When you sign in to your Aleptra account, you gain access to Aleptra's cloud services.

Your Aleptra account lets you manage everything all in one place. You can manage your subscriptions, organize your family's digital life, update your privacy and security settings, track the health of your devices, and earn rewards.

In the cloud, everything is accessible across all your devices, including iOS and Android. Through your Aleptra account, you can connect with the people, content, and entertainment that matter most to you.
